We are DESIGNERS first.

High-end design and animation in advertising is where it all began for us.

Now we team with exhibit developers, fabricators, educators, curators, scientists and historians to create original, immersive digital content for any screen, large or small, interactive or static. Together we combine art, story and technology to promote inspired learning.

DISCOVERY SCIENCE CENTER: Eco Market

California's Discovery Science Center recently opened its Eco Market. This environmentally themed, eco-friendly exhibition features six 3D virtual clerks, two interactive games, and a virtual lobster tank - all designed and animated by Artifact.

MORE

TNT UPFRONT: Large Screen Projections

Turner's Sponsor Shop and designer Misha Mazor harnessed Artifact's skills to create a multi-projector visual experience for TNT's 2011 Upfront presentation in New York City. The stylized content made use of 14 projectors displaying high-res graphics on flowing ribbons around the large auditorium. The dynamic motion-design worked seamlessly to immerse the audience in the TNT brand.

MGM HARMON: Eden Has an Address

Now, imagine entering a room where giant white orchids and rose petals swirl around you playfully, where dreamy images of luxury and nature encircle you in a gentle river of pure tranquility. aaaaah. We combined original photo-realistic 3D animation, original live-action elements, and soothing music to create this HD multi-monitor immersive experience for MGM's luxury high-rise.
